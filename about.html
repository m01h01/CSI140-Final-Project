ABOUT

Mental Health Disorders aften occur in tandem, with on conditions leading to another. To effectly treat these disorders, a treatment must include a dual diagnosis with extensive mental health treatment to begin to heal any emotional or psychological damage.

We have designed a welcoming environment that uses our natural surroundings as a setting for group and individual therapy sessions. Our team is proud to help people identify their issues and walk collaboratively to create a treatment plan to begin a recovery journey.

We help people find their inner strength they need to improve their lives, and always provide high-quality addiction and mental health treatment for people who are ready to make a positive change.

Our Treatment Programs

Everyone is unique, so treatment for disorders should be customized to fit the needs of each individual. Our teams work with each person to make assessment in determining which treatment programs are best for their journey.

Our treatment programs include:

Medical Management

Individual medication management plans begin with a thorough assessment of information such as medical history, amount of drug or alcohol use, and the existing of mental health disorders.

Family Diagnosis

When someone deals with the disorders, relationship with family members are aften damaged. This programming plan helps reestablish connection among family members and explore how to communicate more efficiently with one another.

Relapse Prevention

Our relapse prevention program teaches healthy coping strategies so that our clients won't trap into the same old issues again.
